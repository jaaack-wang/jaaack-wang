I am a PhD candidate at Stony Brook University, advised by Owen Rambow.

I work on evaluation, post-training, multi-modality, and human-AI interaction.

My research connects LLMs to everyday tasks, such as writing (ACL 2025, EMNLP 2025), and referential communication (EMNLP 2025, ACL 2026).

In industry, I have worked on bringing LLMs to customer-facing applications, including topic modeling, ranking, audio processing, and creative ads.
